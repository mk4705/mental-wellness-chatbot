Emotional Regulation Skills

Step 1: Name the Emotion
Label precisely:
Angry, frustrated, disappointed, anxious.

Step 2: Check the Facts
Is the reaction proportional to the event?

Step 3: Opposite Action (when emotion is not helpful)
If anxiety says avoid, take small step forward.
If anger says attack, pause and soften tone.

Step 4: Reduce Vulnerability
Sleep
Eat regularly
Exercise
Limit caffeine

Step 5: Self-Soothing
Use senses:
Music, warm shower, scented candle, calming texture.
