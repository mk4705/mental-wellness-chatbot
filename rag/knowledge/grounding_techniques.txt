Grounding Techniques for Anxiety

Purpose:
Grounding brings attention back to the present moment.

5-4-3-2-1 Method:
5 things you see
4 things you touch
3 things you hear
2 things you smell
1 thing you taste

Temperature Reset:
Hold an ice cube.
Splash cold water on face.
Notice physical sensation.

Body Scan:
Notice tension in each body part.
Relax slowly from head to toe.

Object Focus:
Pick one object.
Describe its color, shape, texture, details.

Breathing Anchor:
Focus fully on inhale and exhale.
If thoughts wander, gently return to breath.
