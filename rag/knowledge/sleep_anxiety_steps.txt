Sleep Anxiety Support Guide

Common Pattern:
Worry → Overthinking → Restlessness → More Anxiety

Step 1: Wind Down Routine
Dim lights 1 hour before bed.
Avoid phone 30 minutes before sleep.

Step 2: Scheduled Worry Time
Set 15 minutes earlier in the evening.
Write all worries down.
Close notebook when time ends.

Step 3: Cognitive Shuffle
Imagine random neutral objects:
Apple → Chair → Blue balloon → Mountain → Coffee mug

Step 4: Breathing Technique
Inhale 4 seconds
Exhale 6 seconds
Repeat slowly.

Step 5: If Awake for 20+ Minutes
Get up briefly.
Do calm activity.
Return to bed when sleepy.

Goal:
Make bed associated with sleep, not stress.
